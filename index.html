// What We Do

Capabilities

A multi-disciplinary practice spanning field geology, spatial analysis, and applied science. Alaska is home base — remote terrain, permafrost, and logistics complexity are the norm, not the exception.

```
📋

Project Management

PMP- and PMI-ACP-certified. Investigation program management, multidisciplinary field team coordination, technical deliverable oversight, and agile project delivery frameworks applied to engineering and science work.

🔩

Subsurface Investigation

Drill program planning and oversight. SPT methodology; hollow-stem auger, rotary, sonic, and cable-tool drilling; test pit supervision; sampling, field logging, and lab test specification. Permafrost and remote-access experience throughout.

🪨

Engineering Geology

USCS field identification; rock mass description per ISRM methods; slope stability and geologic hazard assessment. Alaska context includes glaciomarine silts, Bootlegger Cove Formation, Cook Inlet Quaternary, and Péwé loess deposits.

🛣️

Pavement Evaluation

Pavement distress identification and root-cause analysis. Frost heave and thaw-weakening diagnosis; subgrade characterization; Alaska-specific failure mechanisms from freeze-thaw cycling and permafrost differential settlement.

🗺️

GIS & Spatial Analysis

Geographic information systems, spatial dataset development, cartographic production, and analytical mapping. Remote sensing data integration, raster and vector analysis, and field-to-map workflows for engineering and environmental projects.

🚁

Drone & Remote Sensing

UAS operations; photogrammetric survey and 3D model generation; orthomosaic and DEM production; change detection and site monitoring. Applied to geotechnical, environmental, and infrastructure projects in remote Alaska terrain.

🔭

Research & Applied Science

Independent research in geoscience, planetary science, and applied math. Prior work in asteroid and small-body geology. Current interests include thaw consolidation modeling, regional Alaska geology, and quantitative tools for field practitioners.

🖨️

3D Printing & Fabrication

Meteor Forge Industries — custom 3D printing, prototyping, and small-batch fabrication. FDM and resin. Field instrument mounts, custom parts, models, and whatever else needs to exist in physical form.
```
