05 Sampling Requirements ✓
What sampling is required from the boring?
SPT (Standard Penetration Test)
2" split spoon, blow counts, disturbed sample
Modified California (3" Split Spoon)
Larger sample, common in Alaska — good for gravelly soils
Shelby Tube (Undisturbed)
Soft cohesive soils — needed for strength and consolidation testing
Continuous Sampling
Sample every foot — critical stratigraphy, contamination, or thin layer detection
Rock Core (NQ/HQ/PQ)
Oriented core for RQD, fracture logging, rock strength
No Sample Required
Depth to refusal only, water level, or probe only
