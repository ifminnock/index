PM — Project Management

Project
Management Tools

Interactive tools for PMI certification preparation, meeting facilitation, and organizational planning. Aimed at practitioners working toward or maintaining PMP and PMI-ACP credentials, and at project managers in technical and engineering fields. Most tools in this section are in early development or planned — scope will evolve.

PM — Available Tools
PM01 — Certification
🎯
PMI Event
Prep Wizard

Guided preparation tool for PMI certification events and PDU activities. Walk through event type selection, knowledge domain mapping, and pre-event study focus areas for PMP and PMI-ACP exam alignment. Helps practitioners plan targeted preparation for PDU-eligible events, study groups, and chapter meetings.

PM02 — Facilitation
📋
Meeting Agenda
Builder

Structured agenda development tool for technical project meetings. Define meeting purpose, attendees, agenda items with time allocations, and desired outcomes. Outputs a formatted agenda document ready to distribute. Incorporates PMI-aligned facilitation principles — decision vs. discussion vs. information items clearly distinguished.

Planned Facilitation Meetings PMP

PM03 — Planning
🏗️
Organization
Simulator

Interactive organizational structure modeler for exploring project and program management configurations. Compare functional, matrix, and projectized structures across scenarios. Adjust team size, reporting lines, and resource allocation to visualize authority, communication paths, and span of control. Useful for PMP exam prep and real-world org design exercises.
